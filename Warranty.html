Our Warranty Policy

Manufacterer Warranty:

Many of the products we use come with a manufacturer warranty, and we will honor that warranty in the event of any issues that may arise. We will work with the manufacturer to ensure that any defects are repaired or replaced promptly.

Exclusions:

Our warranty does not cover damage caused by natural disasters, accidents, or other events beyond our control. It also does not cover damage caused by improper maintenance, negligence, or misuse of the roof.

Making a Claim:

To make a warranty claim, please contact us as soon as possible so we can assess the issue and determine the best course of action. We will work with you to resolve any issues in a timely and professional manner

At Ben Scott Roofing, we take great pride in the quality of our work, and we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction. With our comprehensive warranty, you can be confident that your roof will be protected for years to come.
